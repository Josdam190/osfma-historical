HISTORICAL BACKGROUND OF O. SOLID FOUNDATION MEDIA
ACADEMY

This Media Academy started some years ago now and by the grace of God I am as at today part of the story. The Media Academy was a dream of one man who cannot just go all alone in achieving this big dream. It all started with four members and the four members are all Executives. These four members became Pioneer members until today we still refer to them as the pillar of this foundation because of the believe and the faith they have been able to keep in the course of running this Media Academy (O. SOLID FOUNDATION MEDIA ACADEMY).
It all started with the vision of one man and eventually became vision of more than just one man. Speaking about the Pioneer members, OLADIPUPO TOHEEB popularly known as Olakunle Wealth happens to be the carrier of the dream and was made the President and the Director of the Media Academy till date. Odukoya Felix was chosen as the Secretary among the four. The foundation successfully appointed Owoyemi Samuel as their P.R.O. then, so the only female among them all Afolayan Semilore became the welfare Coordinator and this was how it all started. Their first meeting was held in the staff club of the Federal Polytechnic Ilaro, under one big tree like that.
Everyone of them paid a lot of sacrifices, they sacrificed their time, their resources, just to make sure this becomes something worth pointing at. Not quite long, they decided to have their first audition, from whence new members joined the Academy.
